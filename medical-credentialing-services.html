What is Medical Credentialing?

Medical credentialing is the process of verifying and assessing the qualifications, training, and experience of healthcare providers to ensure they meet the standards required by insurance companies, hospitals, and healthcare organizations.

This essential process involves collecting and verifying documents such as medical licenses, board certifications, education history, work experience, malpractice insurance, and references.

Without proper credentialing, healthcare providers cannot bill insurance companies for services rendered, making it a critical step in establishing a successful medical practice.

At CareAxis, we understand the complexities and time-consuming nature of the credentialing process. Our team of experts handles every aspect of credentialing, from initial application to ongoing maintenance, ensuring your providers are always properly credentialed.

We stay current with the constantly changing requirements of various insurance payers and regulatory bodies, reducing the risk of delays or rejections in the credentialing process.
