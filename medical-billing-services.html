About Medical Billing

Understanding the complexities of modern medical billing

Medical billing is the process of submitting and following up on claims with health insurance companies to receive payment for services rendered by healthcare providers. It involves translating healthcare services into billing claims, which are then submitted to payers for reimbursement.

In today's healthcare landscape, medical billing has become increasingly complex with constantly changing regulations, coding requirements, and payer policies. This complexity often leads to claim denials, delayed payments, and lost revenue for medical practices.

At CareAxis, we understand these challenges and have developed sophisticated systems and processes to handle all aspects of medical billing efficiently. Our team of certified billers and coders stays current with all industry changes to ensure maximum reimbursement for your practice.

We handle everything from patient registration and insurance verification to charge entry, payment posting, and accounts receivable follow-up, allowing you to focus on patient care.
