About CareAxis

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to transforming healthcare administration so medical professionals can focus on what matters most - patient care.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, CareAxis began with a simple mission: to alleviate the administrative burden on healthcare providers. What started as a medical billing service has evolved into a comprehensive healthcare solutions provider.

Our journey began when our founder, Dr. Michael Roberts, witnessed firsthand the challenges medical practices faced with revenue cycle management. He saw how administrative complexities were diverting attention from patient care.

Our Approach

At CareAxis, we believe that technology and human expertise should work in harmony. Our solutions combine cutting-edge software with experienced healthcare professionals to deliver exceptional results.

We're not just service providers; we're partners in your success. Our team immerses themselves in your practice to understand your unique challenges and opportunities.

Our Vision

We envision a healthcare ecosystem where administrative tasks no longer hinder medical professionals from delivering exceptional patient care. Our goal is to create seamless, efficient backend processes that empower healthcare providers.

By 2025, we aim to be the leading partner for medical practices seeking to optimize their operations, reduce costs, and enhance patient satisfaction through innovative administrative solutions.

We're committed to continuous innovation, always looking for new ways to simplify complex processes and deliver measurable results for our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare administration by providing comprehensive, technology-driven solutions that maximize revenue, ensure compliance, and streamline operations.

We're dedicated to helping medical practices thrive by handling the complexities of billing, coding, credentialing, and practice management with expertise and precision.

Every day, we work to reduce the administrative burden on healthcare providers, allowing them to focus their time and energy on what truly matters - delivering exceptional patient care.
