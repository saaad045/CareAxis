Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our medical coding services

Our coding team includes professionals with CPC (Certified Professional Coder), CCS (Certified Coding Specialist), CCS-P (Certified Coding Specialist-Physician-based), and other specialty-specific certifications. All coders undergo rigorous training and continuous education to stay current with coding guidelines and regulations.

We employ a multi-layer quality assurance process that includes automated claim scrubbing, peer reviews, and regular audits. Our AI-powered tools flag potential errors before submission, and our quality team performs random audits on 10% of all coded charts to maintain our 97% accuracy standard.

We assign coders with specific expertise to match your practice's specialty. Our coders specialize in areas such as cardiology, orthopedics, oncology, gastroenterology, and other complex specialties. They stay current with specialty-specific coding guidelines and documentation requirements.

Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours for most coding requests. For urgent needs, we offer expedited services with same-day turnaround. We understand the importance of timely coding for cash flow and work to ensure claims are submitted promptly.

We have a dedicated compliance team that monitors all coding updates from CMS, AMA, and other regulatory bodies. Our coders undergo monthly training sessions, and our systems are updated in real-time with coding changes. We also provide regular updates to our clients about important coding changes that affect their practice.

Yes, we are compatible with all major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, and others. We can interface directly with your system or work through secure HL7 connections. Our technical team will work with your IT staff to establish a seamless connection.

We have a streamlined process for querying providers when additional documentation is needed. Our coders use standardized query templates that are respectful of provider time and focused on specific clinical information needed for accurate coding. Queries are tracked and followed up on to ensure timely responses.

We employ enterprise-grade security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Our systems are HIPAA compliant, and all staff undergo rigorous security training. We also sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all our clients.

We track multiple key performance indicators including coding accuracy rate, denial rate, days in A/R, first-pass acceptance rate, and revenue impact. These metrics are included in your monthly performance reports, and we work with you to set and achieve specific goals for your practice.
